Tsunekazu Ishihara

CEO & PRESIDENT

Tsunekazu Ishihara is a Japanese video game designer, director, producer and businessman who is the president of The Pokémon Company.

Ken Sugimori

Art Director

Ken Sugimori is a Japanese video game designer, illustrator, manga artist, and director. He is best known as the primary character designer and art director for the Pokémon franchise.

Satoshi Tajiri

Designer

Satoshi Tajiri is a Japanese video game designer and director best known as the creator of Nintendo's Pokémon franchise and one of the founders, and President of video game developer Game Freak
